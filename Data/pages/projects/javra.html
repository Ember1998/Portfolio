With headquarters in Culemborg, the company has expanded globally with offices in Nepal (since 2006) and Portugal (since 2022), operating on offshore, nearshore, and onshore delivery models to serve diverse client needs. Javra is a Platinum Partner of Pimcore and also collaborates with leading platforms like Adobe and Microsoft Azure. With a team of over 100 professionals, the company focuses on innovation through its dedicated R&D units and is recognized for delivering scalable, enterprise-grade solutions. Beyond technology, Javra runs “Javra Cares,” a social initiative that supports education for underprivileged children in Nepal, reflecting its commitment to both innovation and community development.
Breeding Inventory Application




It improves customer and driver satisfaction by delivering a self-service e-commerce portal that enables users to be in control of their delivery experience.

This project is built on .Net 6 with the integration of serverless AWS lambda as Saas Application. It uses CQRS as design pattern and uses the concept of MediatR. It also uses AUTH0 as authentication and authorization services. React has been used for front end design.

It uses Locus and other third party services for managing the driver and location for delivery services.
